Buffalo Cauliflower Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of Cauliflower
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Package (8 oz.) Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup FRANK'S RedHot Buffalo Wings Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup Ranch Dressing
  • 1/2 Cup Cheddar Cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F. Line a large baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Cut the head of cauliflower into small florets.
  3. Toss the cauliflower with oil and spread cauliflower out evenly on the baking sheet. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cauliflower is slightly charred around the edges. Remove from oven.
  4. Turn oven to 350 F. Transfer cauliflower to a large mixing bowl. Add cream cheese, buffalo sauce, ranch, and cheddar cheese.
  5. Spread mixture out evenly in a baking dish. Top with extra cheese if desired then, bake 25-30 minutes, or until the edges of the dip start to brown.
  6. Remove dip from oven and serve with desired dippers.
